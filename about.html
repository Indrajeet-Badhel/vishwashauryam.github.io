Founded by students who believed that serving the nation is the highest honor, VishwaShauryam has grown from a small study group into VIT Pune's premier defense aspirants community.

Where It All Began

It started when a group of engineering students realized they shared something beyond academics—a calling to serve India. What began as weekend discussions about current affairs and defense strategies in the college canteen has evolved into a structured platform that has guided hundreds of students toward their dreams of joining the armed forces.

Today, we're not just a club; we're a family. A family that celebrates when one of us clears the NDA interview, supports each other through the tough preparation months, and maintains bonds that last long after graduation.
